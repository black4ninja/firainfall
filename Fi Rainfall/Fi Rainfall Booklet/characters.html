Characters

 

Lisa: The oldest of the sisters.

The leader of the group and the one with the bravest heart.

Her ability is velocity instead of magic and force.

Her special attack uses the power of the sun of Sera.

 

 

Darcy: The middle sister.

Is the strongest of them all but her velocity is greatly affected by that.


Her special attack is a combination of the sun and the moon.

 

 

Rita: The youngest of the sisters.

She is more like a crybaby but when it's needed she is very brave.

Eventhough she is fearless, she is the most balanced of the three.

Rita prefers the darkness above the light a feature that comes from her special attack.

Her special attack uses the power of the full moon of Lana.

 

Xerra: The elemental of earth and creator of all fields, mountains and also the humankind.

She has been cursed by the new flame that menaces the universe. She lives in the highest mountain of Ezaye.

Of all the deities she is the most innocent. Her ultimate is rock avalanche.

 

 

Lily: The elemental of water and creator of the oceans in Ezaye.

Her character is cold and and sometimes cruel.

Altough she has been cursed, she uses this poer to destroy all humans that treat her.

She lives in the deepest of the ocean.

Her abilities are changes in the currents.

 

 

Kin: The elemental of fire and protector of Ezaye.

With a deep hatred against humans, she is very powerful, cruel and sometimes sadistic.

Her character influences it's power with more angry she has but also affects how she take the situations without measure the danger.

Her abilities came from flames and lava throw.

 

 

Sera: The most powerful deity of the four. She manages air. Also she is the goddess that defeated the flame long ago becoming the symbol of Sun.

The smartest character of Ezaye, she can summons fairies, fire and magical flames.

In her sleep she was cursed by the flame and because of the time she has been corrupted to the deepest level.

 

 

 

Lana: With the worst character of all. She is jealous about how Sera saved all the universe instead of she.

As the goddess who lived with Sera, she saved her from disappear.

Her abilities are the use of meteorites and moon beams.

Lana is not cursed by the flame but she will test the one who beated Sera.

 

 

 

Rin: The flame that menaces the universe.

It's true form is a girl with 9 tails with the power of all the universe.

She was the creator of Sera and Lana and all that ruled in the darkness before the Universe was born.

Because she was bored she wanted to change all and detroy the universe. Now that her power is back she tries to acomplish what she can't millennias ago.

Her abilities are unknown but it is said that she can mimetize all the habilities from every deity and goddess because they come from her.
