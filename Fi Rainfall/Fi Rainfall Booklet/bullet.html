Bullets

Circle Bullets:

This kind of bullets will increase its size as long as they are on screen. Some of them will go inverse. There are three types Rings, Elipses and a special type that surrounds enemies as a shield.

Direct Bullets:

This kind of bullets will follow the player trouhgout all the screen. As the difficulty is higher more bullets an enemy will cast.

Waterfall Bullets:

This kind of bullets will go from the top of the screen falling as water in one direction like a line.

Rainfall Bullets:

This kind of bullets appear in a random area of the screen and shot several bullets in different directions in a waterfall way but with some bullets at different speed.
